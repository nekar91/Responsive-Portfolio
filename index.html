About Me

I grew up in a small town outside of Houston called Simonton. Simonton was famous for its Saturday night rodeo. Foreign dignitaries and famous people visiting Houston would often be brought out to our rodeo, such as late Chinese Prime Minister Deng Xiaoping and surrealist artist Rene Magritte. I graduated from Texas A&M University with a Bachelor degree in Anthropogy and a Minor in History. After college, I worked as a job coach for adults with disablities. I traveled around Europe for a few months, then came back to Texas and moved to Austin. In Austin, I continued to work with adults with disabilities, both as a job coach and as a case manager in a guardianship program. Later, I worked for the Texas Real Estate Commission/Texas Appraiser Licensing and Certification Board as an Education Specialist.
